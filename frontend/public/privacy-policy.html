Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

White Label Shipping Bot ("we", "our", or "bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Telegram bot service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our bot, we collect the following information:

  • Telegram Account Information: User ID, username, and first name
  • Shipping Information: Sender and recipient addresses, including names, street addresses, cities, states, and ZIP codes
  • Order Details: Parcel weight, dimensions, carrier selection, and service type
  • Payment Information: Transaction amounts, payment dates, and cryptocurrency payment details
  • Bot Interaction Data: Commands used, messages sent, and interaction history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage shipping labels through our shipping service providers
  • To process payments securely through our payment gateway partners
  • To send order status updates and notifications
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To maintain and improve service quality and security
  • To store order templates for your convenience

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely in encrypted databases
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Payment information is processed through third-party payment providers
  • We do not store credit card information or sensitive payment credentials
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your information only with trusted third-party service providers necessary for our operations:

  • Shipping Service Providers: Shipping information is shared to create and manage shipping labels
  • Payment Processors: Payment transaction data is processed for secure cryptocurrency payments

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data for marketing purposes
  • Disclose your information except as required by law

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data stored in our system
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of non-essential notifications (essential order updates will still be sent)

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Order history and transaction records are retained for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the bot. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us through the bot's contact administrator option.

By using White Label Shipping Bot, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
